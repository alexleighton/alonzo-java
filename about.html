About Alonzo

Alonzo was conceived of while reading a comment on Hacker News. The comment asked for a project that showed how one goes about implementing a functional language at a low level and I thought "hey, I can do that", and "that sounds like a lot of fun, actually." So, I created this project to scratch a few itches and have some fun.
